Gochujang Noodles

Creamy, buttery noodles recipe

Perfect for college students or cooking-phobic people in need of an easy 10-15 min recipe! This savory meal only needs a few ingredients to enlighten your taste buds.

Follow the guide below to make your very own creamy udon noodles at home üçú

Ingredients

  • udon noodles
  • butter
  • mushrooms
  • gochujang (ofc)
  • garlic
  • brown sugar
  • soy sauce
  • heavy cream

How-to

  1. Thaw your frozen udon in boiling water and set aside
    • Should only take 2-3 min for noodles to completely thaw
  2. Mince garlic to your liking and set aside
  3. Coat your pan with butter
  4. Add mushrooms and chopped garlic to the pan. Mix evenly
  5. Add in heavy cream, gochujang, brown sugar, and soy sauce
    • Up to your preference/taste on portion size for sauces just like any good ol' Asian recipe
  6. Add Udon noodles to pan
  7. Stir noodles and mix evenly with sauce

Optional

  • Add bacon bits and sesame seeds for garnish

And that's it! Now you have your very own savory meal for when you need a quick bite or a lift-me-up when you're contemplating life.
