Corn Cheese

Gloriously cheesy and golden

This classic corn cheese recipe is a korean staple that you can now make in your very own kitchen. It is a simple, easy recipe that makes for the perfect snack.

Ingredients

  • sweet corn
  • cheese
  • condensed milk
  • kewpie mayo
  • butter
  • salt and pepper
  • mozzarella cheese slice
  • optional: parsley for garnish

How to make

  1. Thaw sweet corn if applicable
  2. Mix condensed milk and sweet corn in a separate bowl
  3. Add salt and pepper to the mixture
  4. Melt butter on pan and add corn mix
  5. Drizzle kewpie mayo on corn mix. Stir evenly
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese and garnish
